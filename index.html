ABOUT LIMCOMA

A Farmer’s Dream Since 1970

LIMCOMA operates modern facilities, breeding farms, and satellite offices while providing high-quality feeds, accessible loan programs, scholarships, and community support initiatives. More than just a feedmill, LIMCOMA remains committed to uplifting farmers, strengthening communities, and turning the dream of a better life into reality for its members..

  • Farmer-owned and operated cooperative
  • High-quality, affordable feed production
  • Low-interest agricultural loans
